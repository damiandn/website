About Me

Damian Dalle Nogare

I am a microscopist, bioimage analyst, and cell and developmental biologist working at the National Institutes of Health in Bethesda, MD. My areas of expertise are 3D time-lapse microscopy, quantitative image analysis, super-resolution microscopy, molecular-genetics, and experimental cell biology, primarily in the zebrafish model system.
